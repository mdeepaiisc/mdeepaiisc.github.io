I am Deepa Mishra, a research scholar (Ph.D.) at the department of the Materials Research Centre, Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore.

I have completed my schooling, both high school, and secondary high school from the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) board. I have completed my Post-graduation, Integrated M. Tech, in Biomedical Engineering from Indian Institute of Information Technology, Allahabad (UP). I joined Ph.D. at the Materials Research Centre, IISc Bangalore in 2019 (August) under the supervision of Prof Bikramjit Basu. Upon joining IISc, I received the Prime Minister’s Research Fellowship through lateral mode in my second year.

Currently, I am working on the Design and Development of both ceramic and metallic dental implant systems in close collaboration with clinicians (prosthodontics) to closely understand the clinical insights of each of the dental implant systems.
